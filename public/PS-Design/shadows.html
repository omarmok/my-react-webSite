Shadows

Shadow tokens define elevation levels used across cards, menus, and overlays.

Overview

Use shadow tokens to communicate elevation and hierarchy. All values are defined in _variables.scss.

Accessibility

  • Use elevation cues to communicate interactive surfaces.
  • Ensure shadow contrast remains visible against the background.

Usage Guidelines

Low elevation

  • Use $shadow-xs or $shadow-sm for cards and sections.
  • Reserve higher levels for overlays.

High elevation

  • Use $shadow-xl and above for modals or floating menus.
  • Keep elevation consistent within a flow.

Do and Do Not

Do

  • Use shadow tokens to reinforce hierarchy.
  • Match elevation levels to interaction importance.

Do Not

  • Do not stack shadows outside the defined scale.
  • Do not rely on shadows alone for affordance.

Code Examples

.card {
  box-shadow: $shadow-sm;
}

.modal {
  box-shadow: $shadow-xl;
}
